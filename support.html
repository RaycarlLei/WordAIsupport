Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Effective Date: January 2025

1. Introduction

WordAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). This policy complies with applicable U.S. privacy laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and other federal and state privacy regulations.

By using WordAI, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, authentication credentials (when you create an account using email/password)
  • Account Identifiers: User ID, account creation timestamp, last login timestamp
  • Third-Party Account Information: When you sign in using Google, Apple, or GitHub, we receive basic profile information (name, email) as provided by those services

2.2 Usage Information

  • Vocabulary Searches: Words and phrases you search for in the app (processed in real-time, not stored)
  • App Preferences: Language settings, starred words, and other user preferences
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version (for technical support and app improvement)

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Crash Reports: Error logs and crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics) to help us improve app stability
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics (via Firebase Performance) to optimize user experience

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following business purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide vocabulary definitions, translations, and language learning features
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, authenticate your identity, and provide account-related services
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, improve app functionality, and develop new features
  • Technical Support: To respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and provide customer support
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or security threats
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

4. How We Collect Data

We collect information through the following methods:

  • Directly from You: When you create an account, use search features, or contact support
  • Third-Party Authentication Services: Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, and GitHub Sign-In (we receive only the information you authorize these services to share)
  • Automated Technologies: Through Firebase services (Authentication, Crashlytics, Performance) and app analytics
  • OpenAI API: When you search for word definitions, your search query is sent to OpenAI's API to retrieve vocabulary meanings. OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Account Data: Stored securely using Firebase Authentication and Firestore. We retain account information until you delete your account.
  • Search Queries: Vocabulary search terms are processed in real-time and are not stored or retained by our systems.
  • Local Data: Starred words and preferences are stored locally on your device and synced to your account when signed in.
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 90 days for troubleshooting purposes.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

6.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share information with the following categories of third-party service providers who assist in operating our App:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database storage, crash reporting, and performance monitoring
  • OpenAI: For processing vocabulary search queries and retrieving word definitions
  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app purchases and subscriptions (if applicable)

These service providers are contractually obligated to use your information only for the purposes we specify and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

6.2 No Sale of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy.

6.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

隐私政策

最后更新： 2025年1月

生效日期： 2025年1月

1. 简介

WordAI（"我们"、"我们的"或"我们"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们在您使用我们的移动应用程序（"应用"）时如何收集、使用、披露和保护您的信息。本政策符合适用的美国隐私法律，包括《加州消费者隐私法》（CCPA）和其他联邦和州隐私法规。

使用WordAI即表示您同意本政策中描述的数据做法。如果您不同意本政策，请不要使用我们的应用。

2. 我们收集的信息

2.1 个人信息

我们收集以下类别的个人信息：

  • 账户信息： 邮箱地址、身份验证凭据（当您使用邮箱/密码创建账户时）
  • 账户标识符： 用户ID、账户创建时间戳、最后登录时间戳
  • 第三方账户信息： 当您使用Google、Apple或GitHub登录时，我们会收到这些服务提供的基本个人资料信息（姓名、邮箱）

2.2 使用信息

  • 词汇搜索： 您在应用中搜索的词汇和短语（实时处理，不存储）
  • 应用偏好： 语言设置、收藏词汇和其他用户偏好
  • 设备信息： 设备类型、操作系统版本、应用版本（用于技术支持和应用改进）

2.3 自动收集的信息

  • 崩溃报告： 错误日志和崩溃报告（通过Firebase Crashlytics）以帮助我们提高应用稳定性
  • 性能数据： 应用性能指标（通过Firebase Performance）以优化用户体验

3. 我们如何使用您的信息

我们将收集的信息用于以下商业目的：

  • 服务交付： 提供词汇定义、翻译和语言学习功能
  • 账户管理： 创建和管理您的账户，验证您的身份，并提供账户相关服务
  • 服务改进： 分析使用模式，改进应用功能，并开发新功能
  • 技术支持： 回应您的询问，排除故障，并提供客户支持
  • 安全： 检测、预防和解决技术问题、欺诈或安全威胁
  • 法律合规： 遵守适用的法律、法规和法律程序

4. 我们如何收集数据

我们通过以下方法收集信息：

  • 直接从您： 当您创建账户、使用搜索功能或联系支持时
  • 第三方身份验证服务： Google登录、Apple登录和GitHub登录（我们仅收到您授权这些服务共享的信息）
  • 自动化技术： 通过Firebase服务（身份验证、Crashlytics、Performance）和应用分析
  • OpenAI API： 当您搜索词汇定义时，您的搜索查询会发送到OpenAI的API以检索词汇含义。OpenAI根据其隐私政策处理此数据。

5. 数据存储和保留

  • 账户数据： 使用Firebase身份验证和Firestore安全存储。我们保留账户信息直到您删除账户。
  • 搜索查询： 词汇搜索词实时处理，我们的系统不存储或保留。
  • 本地数据： 收藏词汇和偏好存储在您的设备本地，并在登录时同步到您的账户。
  • 崩溃报告： 保留最多90天用于故障排除。

  • RevenueCat： 用于管理应用内购买和订阅（如适用）

这些服务提供商有合同义务仅将您的信息用于我们指定的目的，并符合本隐私政策。
